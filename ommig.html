Elif, born 1998 and studying 1st semester as a Multimedia designer in Zealand, Køge - Denmark. I live in Copenhagen - Taastrup. In my free time besides school I have interests in travelling around the world, exploring different places in the world, researching a lot, improving myself more creatively. I am not fond of colours in photography. Black and white photography makes me feel more "calm" - and I always believe that there is a beauty and a history behind the photos. And you can see my point of view of photography on the first page of my portfolio.

I have always been interested in photography. Since 2015 I have been taking pictures from my iPhone 5s and it has evolved. I bought my first camera Canon 700D late in 2016. I am very much in favor of the so-called "street photography", objects and buildings. First time in year 2017 have I taken photos of the couple, as you can see on my first page. I prefer to avoid faces on my photographies - but I will never say no to a challenge with colours and faces. It's important for me to try my best and take challenges.

I discovered that I was creative when I started as a Multimedia designer. So I do not want put myself inside a box and just say "I'm only good at photography" - no! I am open to everything. Under my projects you would also be able to see what other creative work I do.

I really want to collaborate with others who are passionate about what they love to do. If you want to work with me then contact me!
